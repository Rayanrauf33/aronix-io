## Section: The problem

Label: What's happening on your website right now

The average service business website converts between 1% and 3% of visitors into enquiries. That means 97 out of every 100 people who find you through Google, your ads, or a referral leave without making contact.

Most of them had a question they wanted answered. Your prices, your availability, whether you cover their area. They didn't find the answer fast enough, so they left.

A contact form doesn't fix this. It asks for information and makes them wait. People in buying mode don't wait.

## Section: What we build

Label: What we build

We build an AI chat agent that lives on your website and knows your business properly. Not a scripted button tree that dead-ends after three clicks. A conversational agent that can answer real questions, handle objections, and guide a visitor from curious to booked.

It knows your services, your pricing ranges, your location, your availability, and the most common questions your customers ask. If someone asks whether you cover their postcode, it knows. If they ask how long a job takes, it knows.

When a visitor is ready to book, the agent takes them straight into your calendar. Real slots, no back and forth, no "we'll be in touch." The appointment lands in your calendar, and you get a notification.

For visitors who aren't ready yet, it captures their contact details and hands them off to your follow-up sequence automatically. No lead falls through.

## Section: FAQ

Q: Is this just a basic chatbot with preset answers?
A: No. It's a conversational AI agent that handles open-ended questions, not a button tree. It can respond to questions it wasn't explicitly trained on by reasoning from your knowledge base, the same way a well-briefed member of staff would.

Q: What if it gets something wrong?
A: Every conversation is logged. If it gives a wrong answer, we catch it in the monthly review and update the knowledge base so it doesn't happen again. For anything genuinely sensitive: pricing disputes, complaints: we set escalation rules so those conversations route to you directly.

Q: Can it book into my existing calendar system?
A: Yes, as long as your calendar has an integration available. We check this during the audit. The common ones: Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity: all connect cleanly.

Q: Will visitors know they're talking to an AI?
A: We configure the agent to be transparent about being an AI assistant where that's the right call for your market. Some businesses prefer this framed as a virtual assistant. We discuss this with you during setup and handle it in a way that fits your brand.

Q: Can it handle multiple languages?
A: Yes. If a meaningful portion of your website visitors aren't native English speakers, we can configure the agent to detect the language and respond accordingly. Worth raising during the audit if this applies to you.

Q: What happens when I add new services or change my pricing?
A: You let us know and we update the knowledge base. That's included in the monthly management. You're not maintaining a complex system yourself.
