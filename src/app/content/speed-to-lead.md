## Section: The problem

Label: Why speed matters more than anything else

A Harvard Business Review study found that responding to a lead within 5 minutes makes you 100 times more likely to connect compared to waiting 30 minutes. Most businesses take 47 hours to respond. By then, the lead has already booked with someone else.

The problem isn't that you don't want to follow up fast. It's that you're with a customer, on a job, or simply didn't see the notification in time. Manual follow-up is always going to lose to a system that responds in seconds.

78% of deals go to the first vendor to respond. That's not a sales technique. It's just math.

## Section: What we build

Label: What we build

We build an automated response system that triggers the moment a new lead comes in, from any source. Web form, Facebook lead ad, Google ad, WhatsApp message, Instagram DM, missed call: it doesn't matter. The system picks it up and responds within seconds.

The first message is personalised to the lead source and what they asked about. It's not a generic "thanks for your enquiry." It references what they filled in and asks the one question that moves the conversation forward.

From there, the system handles the back-and-forth. It qualifies the lead, answers basic questions, and either books them into your calendar or flags them to you when the conversation needs a human. You see the full thread. You can jump in at any point.

If a lead goes quiet, the system follows up. Once after 24 hours, again at 72 hours. Not in an annoying way: just a short, direct nudge that gets replies. Most leads that go cold just needed one more touch.

## Section: FAQ

Q: What channels can it respond through?
A: SMS, email, and WhatsApp are the main three. If your leads come through a specific platform: Facebook, Instagram, a CRM: we connect to that too. We map everything in the audit before we build.

Q: Will it sound automated?
A: Not if we set it up properly. The messages are written in your voice, based on real enquiries your business gets. Short, direct, and personal. Most people assume it's you or your team replying.

Q: What if a lead asks something complicated?
A: The system handles common questions automatically. Anything outside that, it flags to you with the full conversation thread so you can reply with context. You never walk into a conversation blind.

Q: Can I connect it to my existing CRM?
A: Usually yes. We've worked with HubSpot, GoHighLevel, and several others. We check compatibility during the audit. If your CRM can't connect, we'll tell you upfront.

Q: What happens after the first follow-up sequence?
A: If a lead hasn't responded after the follow-up sequence, we can either stop or move them into a longer nurture sequence: monthly touchpoints to stay in front of them until they're ready. We set that up based on what makes sense for your sales cycle.

Q: How do I know it's working?
A: The dashboard shows you every lead, every message sent, and every reply received. We also review the conversion data monthly and show you what's booking versus what's dropping off.
