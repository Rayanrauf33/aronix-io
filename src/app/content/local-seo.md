## Section: The problem

Label: What happens when you're not ranking locally

Local search is where buying intent is highest. Someone searching for a plumber, a dental clinic, or an electrician in their area is ready to book, not browsing. If your business isn't showing in the local pack: the three results with the map on Google: they're calling one of the businesses that is.

Most local businesses aren't ranking because of problems that are completely fixable. An incomplete Google Business Profile. Inconsistent name, address, and phone number across directories. A website that loads slowly or has no local content. These aren't technical mysteries: they're maintenance problems that compound over time when nobody's paying attention to them.

The businesses ranking at the top aren't necessarily better than you. They've just done the work to be more visible.

## Section: What we do

Label: What we work on

We start with a full audit of where you currently stand: your Google Business Profile, your local rankings for the searches that matter to your business, your citations across directories, and the local SEO foundations on your website.

From that audit, we work through a priority-ordered set of improvements. Google Business Profile is usually first because it has the biggest impact in the shortest time. Profile completeness, categories, photos, review management, and posts: most businesses are leaving easy ranking improvements on the table here.

We then work through citation consistency, making sure your business name, address, and phone number are correct and identical across the directories that matter. Inconsistencies here actively hurt your rankings.

On the website side, we handle the technical and content foundations. Location pages, local schema markup, page speed, and the on-page elements Google uses to decide whether your website is relevant to a local search.

Review generation is part of the work too. Google ranks businesses with more recent, higher-rated reviews higher. We set up the systems that make it easy for happy customers to leave a review without you having to remember to ask.

## Section: FAQ

Q: How long until I see results?
A: The foundation fixes: Google Business Profile, citations, technical issues: typically show ranking improvement within a few weeks. Moving into the top three of the local pack takes longer in competitive markets, typically a few months of consistent work. We'll give you a realistic expectation for your specific market during the audit.

Q: We've tried SEO before and it didn't work. Why would this be different?
A: The most common reason local SEO doesn't work is that the work stops too early or the wrong things are prioritised. A one-off set of changes doesn't maintain rankings: Google's algorithm updates regularly, competitors keep working, and reviews keep accumulating. We do this as an ongoing engagement for exactly that reason.

Q: Do we need to be on social media for this to work?
A: No. Local SEO is specifically about Google Search and Google Maps. Social media presence can support it but isn't required. We focus on what drives calls from local search intent.

Q: Can you help with Google Ads as well?
A: Local SEO and Google Ads serve different purposes. Local SEO builds organic visibility over time. Ads get you to the top immediately but stop the moment you stop paying. Many businesses use both. If you're asking whether we manage Google Ads campaigns, ask us on the call and we'll point you in the right direction.

Q: What if we have multiple locations?
A: Multi-location local SEO is more complex but follows the same principles. Each location needs its own Google Business Profile, its own location page on the website, and its own citation consistency. We scope multi-location work separately.

Q: How do we get more Google reviews?
A: We set up a review generation system: usually a simple automated sequence that goes out to customers after a job is complete, with a direct link to leave a review. Most businesses that implement this see their review count double within a couple of months. We manage this as part of the monthly service.
